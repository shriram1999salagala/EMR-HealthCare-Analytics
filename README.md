# EMR-HealthCare-Analytics
Analyzed patient, treatment, and hospital operational data from Electronic Medical Records (EMR) to identify trends in patient care and resource utilization.
Developed interactive dashboards to monitor key healthcare KPIs such as patient admissions, discharge rates, length of stay, disease distribution, and doctor performance.
Performed data cleaning, transformation, and validation to improve data quality and ensure accurate reporting for healthcare stakeholders.
Generated actionable insights through trend analysis and visualization, supporting data-driven decisions to improve patient outcomes and operational efficiency.
Collected, cleaned, and analyzed healthcare datasets to identify patterns in patient demographics, diagnoses, and treatment outcomes.
